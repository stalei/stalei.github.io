Hi there

My name is Shahram Talei. I am a PhD candiate in computational astrophysics at the university of Alabama. I got my bachelor in solid state physics and my master in GR meanwhile I worked as a commercial programmer for many years ( mainly visual studio and sql server). In 2016 I moved to the UA and started learning about cosmological simulations. I am mainly interested in stellar halo simulations.

I mainly use collisionless N-body codes like Gadget and coupling them with semi-analytic models like Sage, to create a self-consistent simulation of the stellar halo.

My personal interests:

  • Galaxy formation and evolution
  • Cosmological simulations- Dark matter simulations- subgrid models
  • Scientific computing- high performance computing (parallel programming techniques like MPI)
  • Stellar halo models and particle tagging
  • Semi-analytic models
  • Hiking and photography!
